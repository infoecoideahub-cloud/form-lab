 How We Help 

You bring the idea. We help you make it real.

Step 1 — Fill the form & upload your files

Tell us what you want to print, answer a few simple questions, and upload one or multiple 3D files.

Step 2 - We prepare your model

You upload a ready file — we prepare it for printing and confirm all details before production.

Step 3 — Confirm price & pay

The final price depends on size, quantity, and colors.
We’ll send you a secure payment link after reviewing your request.

Step 4 — We print & ship

We print your model and ship it safely to your address anywhere in the USA.
Shipping is paid by the customer.
